T/F

Leading is The horizontal distance between baselines of type.

T/F

False. Leading is The vertical distance between baselines of type.

Which one?

What is the space between letters?

  • A. Kerning
  • B. Leading

  • C. Tracking
  • D. Spacing

Which one?

A. Kerning!

Short Ans

What does the term x-height refer to?

Short Ans

The height of a lowercase character, usually the x, from the baseline to the top of character.

Bonus Q

Very small text (5 points or less) needs what to make it most legible?

Bonus Q

Additional overall letterspacing.

Short Ans

What does the term monospace refer to? (Hint: code!).

Short Ans

Each character is the same horizontal width.

T/F

Typeface = Font ?

T/F

Nooo. False. Typeface (or type family) is the name of a specific collection of related fonts.

Which one?

____ typeface is good for headings.

  • A. Capation
  • B. Display

  • C. Script
  • D. Head

Which one?

B. Display!
